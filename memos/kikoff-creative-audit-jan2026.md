   "Denied again?" → "Here's the fix" formula is working

*3. Use large UI B-roll liberally*
   Full screen app demos, screen replacements — viewers want to SEE the product

*4. Green keywords = attention*
   16 winners use green/red text highlighting for key benefits

*5. Test more creator partnerships*
   Anthony O'Neal has 3 winners — find more finance-adjacent creators

*6. Seasonal content performed well*
   Q4 themes (Halloween, Christmas, NYE) all produced winners

*7. Diagnose the December dip*
   Only 4 winners vs. 24 in Sept — volume issue? Creative fatigue?
